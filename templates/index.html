How to use OxLet:
  1. Type your new words to the textbox below.
    The format is one word/phrase per line.
    Example:
    • sustainable
    • set off
    • horrible
  2. Click Submit.
How to import this result to Quizlet:

  1. Copy everything on the textbox below.
  2. Open Quizlet (you might have to log in before moving on).
  3. Select "Create". Then, Study set. Then, Import from Word, Excel, Google Docs, etc.
  4. After the previous step, a new window appears.
    Paste the result which you copied in step 1 to the textbox on the new window.
  5. Below that textbox, there are some radius buttons.
    • In the Between term and definition section, you need to choose the third button (which is called custom) and type | (the vertical bar which often resides above your Enter key on your keyboard).
    • In the Between cards section, you also select the third button (which is also called custom) and type \n\n.
  6. Finally, click Import.
